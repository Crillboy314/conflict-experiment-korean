  2. If Second Person chooses B, then you will receive 70 points and Second Person will receive 20 points.
On the other hand, when you choose B, then the follow outcomes are possible:
  1. If Second Person chooses A, then you will receive 20 points and Second Person will receive 70 points.
  2. If Second Person chooses B, then you will both receive 40 points each.
